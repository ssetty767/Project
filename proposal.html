Project Proposal

I'd like to create a website for people to get an overview of cricket. It's a very popular sport outside the US, but many in the US don't know it or understand how it works.

I have a lot of friends that ask me how cricket works given they weren't exposed to it and this site could serve as a resource for people to understand the game.

Target audience is people primarily in the US. International folks have pretty good exposure to cricket given it's spread, but in the US it hasn't caught on as much.

Content on each page

  • Batting - similiar to baseball when you are trying to score runs
  • Bowling - similiar to pitching in baseball
  • Fielding
  • Noteworthy performances
  • Cricket in the US
